Get to know me!

Hello! I am Sandeep K, a Senior Specialist Engineer (UX/UI) based in Bengaluru, India. My interest in the web developed back in my sophomore year of engineering @NITRourkela, trying to visualize protein and genome family trees using Canvas (and later D3). Began my career as tech evangelist of Java-based stacks (JSP/JSF era) and later moved away when NodeJS disrupted front end development.

I have had the previlige working at an IT Services major, a Telecom corporation and with various start-ups helping them build mission critical solutions. I am an occasional speaker, people manager and trainer for teams at budding startups with passionate graduates getting introduced to IT world or experienced professionals alike.

Currently I am focused on architecting scalable, accessible, sustainable and performant tech stacks with improved user and developer experience. Feel free to contact me here.

Twist UI Design System

@BT Global

Twist is a collection of tools, concepts, and best practices that helps the teams within BT Global to produce enterprise digital products using high-quality web interfaces at scale.

It extends BT Brand Design Language and consists of reusable components catering to three major Javascript frameworks, an icon library, and form validation architecture with a built-in ruleset, offering top-notch developer experience to build applications rapidly.
